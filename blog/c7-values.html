My favorite value, and its not on the list, is Harmony.

I know, how hippy-dippy does that sound?

But I believe the thing that makes people different from the other apes is that we can cooperate like nothing else on earth.

Oh sure; war and history and religion and all that..
but you should see what baboons do to each other.
Really, there is just no comparison.
Sure you always hear about the loving way of the Bonobos, but that's when they have plenty to eat,
you should see those dudes when there is a resource scarcity.
Not so much with the mutual grooming then.

Primatologists will tell you, genetically, we are functionally the same as the apes, but man did you see that satellite land on that comet?

How's your space program going chimpanzee's?

I feel I have drifted. Oh right Harmony.

I'm not saying every second of everyday has to be spent striving to cooperate better with every single person around us. Just don't add a bunch of unnecessary friction the world.

If you need to shut up for ten minutes about the supremacy of Sam Adam's beer when you meet your fiance's brother for the first time...

What is to be gained, by dissing Leinenkugel's.

(First the monkey thing and the then the beer thing? Two metaphors? That's just bad writing.)
