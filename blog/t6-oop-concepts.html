Inheritance vs Composition

The difference between composition and inheritance is, from my current vantage point; pretty subtle.

We use inheritance to group similar objects together. Sub classes inherit methods with super classes.

Composition accomplishes something similar with by creating new classes and libraries of methods and constants called Modules.

It might boil down to a personal style or team culture

It might also run afoul of the Agile community.

I can't wait to see.
